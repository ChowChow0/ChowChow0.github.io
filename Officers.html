Beyan Kesselly
 Beyan Kesselly is our school chapter's president. With the growing amount of interest in the STEM field, Beyan joined TSA to increase his knowledge about the STEM field. With his vast experience and knowledge after participating in the competition numerous years (as he is a senior right now), Beyan has provided his fellow competitors and officers with valuable knowledge that has helped others expand their interests and knowledge in the STEM field. Throughout his TSA career, Beyan has participated in numerous events and has placed numerous times making him a highly-respected officer in our chapter.

John Qian
 Our school’s TSA chapter’s Vice President is John Qian. John enjoys being an officer for TSA because he believes that STEM is crucial in the current age as technology is constantly improving and new discoveries are constantly being found, so there’s a lot that the current generation should do to improve their future and the future of future generations. He placed 2nd during the 2019 Delaware States competition for Video Game Design with Jonas Ho (current Secretary), Taran Venkatesh (current Treasurer), and Ashwin Sawant (current Sergeant-at-Arms).

Jonas Ho
 The chapter's treasurer is Jonas Ho. Jonas has aspired to be in the STEM field because he believes that STEm teaches students what they need to know to make impactful discoveries as well as promoting advancements in technology. He's grateful to be part of a school that prominently features STEM, thus he wanted to take advantage of that opportunity and become as involved as he could in STEM-related activities, TSA included. Jonas has placed 2nd during 2019 Delaware States competition for Video Game Design with John Qian (current Vice President), Taran Venkatesh (current Treasurer), and Ashwin Sawant (current Sergeant-at-Arms)

Taran Venkatesh
 Taran Venkatesh is our TSA chapter’s treasurer. Taran joined TSA because he is passionate about engineering as it allows students hands-on experience while also providing them confidence to design, build, and test objects independently outside of school. He has placed 2nd during 2019 Delaware States competition for Video Game Design with John Qian (current Vice President), Jonas Ho (current Secretary), and Ashwin Sawant (current Sergeant-at-Arms).

Flynn Miller
 Flynn Miller is our chapter's Reporter. Flynn joined TSA because he always desired having an occupation in the engineering field, and TSA provided him with an opportunity to sharpen his skills. Using the welcome he received from the TSA community, Flynn joined to become an officer because it gave him the chance to offer advice, help, and encouragement to his fellow members to achieve their goals and perform within their respective competitions. Despite this year being Flynn's first year in TSA, he has proved to be the encouraging and talented officer he has aimed to be.
