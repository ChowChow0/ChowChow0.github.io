Meetings

Meetings are held twice a week on Wednesdays and Thursdays during activity period. Our activity period is a 30 minute period of time in the middle of every school day. The purpose of activity period is to give students the opportunity to attend their clubs, go in for extra help, and make up work for the classes they missed. For TSA specifically, students are given the opportunity to go in twice a week during activity period, but also after school from around 2:25 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. TSA members are required to attend at least one of these meetings each week. During these meetings, students will work on their events, either with their group or individually.

Accountability

To keep track of attendance, there is a sign in sheet that is passed around at the beginning of each meeting so students can sign in and keep track of the meetings theyâ€™ve attended. In addition, at the beginning of each month, there is a check-in form where students must update the teacher on the status of the event and the progress made since the previous month. After they are completed, students check in face-to-face with the TSA officer who is assigned to their event. During this check-in, officers assess the progress each student or team has made in their event and make sure that they know what the deadlines and expectations are.

State Competition

Our school participates in two competitions. The first one is the State Conference which will be held at Delaware Technical Community College on April 1-2 of 2020. The competition brings together numerous students with a passion for STEM along with many advisors, judges, and chapters from across the state.

National Competition

The second competition our chapter participates in is the National Conference which will be held at Gaylord Opryland Resort and Convention Center in Nashville, TN from June 27-July 1, 2020. Only those who placed 1st, 2nd, or 3rd in the state will be eligible to compete in the national competition.
