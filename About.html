"We are on a mission: to save and protect, to invent and inspire, and to build a better future."

When we first started Electrx, we envisioned a world where cars relied solely on electricity without gas, without carbon emissions, and without harming the earth. We came together in highschool, and through our passion for electric cars and our vision to make the world a better place, we built our very first manufacturing facility in our hometown of Newark, Delaware. From there, we continued to grow and improve, as well as set up Electrx dealerships throughout the U.S. Soon, our company became part of a billion dollar industry.

As of now we are solely based in the United States, but we plan to expand with branches in China, the U.A.E, and all over the world.
